Ingredients:
 - 1 loaf of sourdough bread
 - 1 cup parmesean cheese
 - 1 8 oz can of tomato sauce



Directions:
 - Turn on oven and set to broil
 - cut bread loaf in half length wise
 - place bread on baking sheet or pan
 - place in oven for 3 min or until toasted.
 - pull bread out of oven and apply tomato sauce liberally
 - apply parmesean cheese liberally
 - and peper or garlic to taste
 - put back in oven for 2 min or until parmesean melts (Yes it melts)
 - remove from oven and cut into slices
 - turn off oven
